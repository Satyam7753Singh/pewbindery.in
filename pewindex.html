Binding and Printing Machine Manufacturer.

Pramod Engineering, is a fast growing printing and post-press (binding) machinery/ equipment manufacturing Company. We also do general engineering and prototyping.

The Company is committed to manufacture international standard cost-effective post-press machines and maintain quality and reliability. Pramod Engineering is presently involved in the manufacturing of Flow-line with 4-auto-feeders + stitcher in-line ejector & Three-Side-trimmer; Stitch-Star stand alone double-head DH II; Stitch-Star flow-line multi-head stitcher; Glory Signature Gatherer up to 30 stations; Stitch-star signature gatherer; Complete wire-stitching comprising of automatic section feeder, reject-copy ejector and in-line three-Side trimmer; Pride Web-line Stacker; Trim Star Three-Side Trimmer; besides Paper Drilling Machine.

It’s a part of Delhi Press magazines (est. 1938), which is the publisher of 32 magazines in 9 languages. 4 million magazine copies are printed in the state-of-the-art presses of Delhi Press and its here where the genesis of Pramod Engineering is.
